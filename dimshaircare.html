About the Studio

Crafted with Passion

Based in Manchester, Dimshaircare is a specialist hair studio dedicated to celebrating the beauty and versatility of Black and textured hair. Every client leaves feeling confident and seen.

8+ Years of Excellence
Over eight years of hands-on experience crafting beautiful hairstyles for clients across Manchester and beyond.
Flexible Appointments
Evening and weekend slots available. We work around your schedule, not the other way around.
Premium Hair Products
Only the finest braiding hair, extensions, and wig materials are used — gentle on your scalp and long-lasting.
For All Hair Types
Dimshaircare serves clients of all hair types, textures, and backgrounds — everyone is welcome here.
