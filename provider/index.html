How this dashboard fits the launch process
  1. After you apply, the operator publishes you under Applications → Publish to site with a listing ID and your email.
  2. Customers book your card on the marketplace; you get a thread here to send a price offer (or accept their counter).
  3. They pay from their dashboard once a price is agreed. Card payouts use Stripe Connect if the operator set your listing ID up there.
  4. If you see no requests yet, you may still be on seed/placeholder listings only — ask the operator to publish your profile.
