FAQ

How do I pay?

Secure card checkout via Stripe. You’ll receive confirmation by email and can track orders from your account flow where enabled.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Delivery options are set by each store. Many offer collection or local delivery first; we’re expanding UK coverage as stores join.

How do I sell on Clip Services?

Apply from List your store. We review listings, verify identity, and publish your catalogue — usually within 48 hours.
